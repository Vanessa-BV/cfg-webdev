Run Amsterdam! ❤

Welcome to runner'dam. I am Vanessa, and I would love to share my love for running in Amsterdam with you. Having lived in the city for over 3 years and having completed some half- and full marathon trainings here, I have seen many spots in the city. It never gets boring running in Amsterdam, as the city gives you so much diversity from running in the city center along the canals or enjoying some nature in the Amsterdamse Bos. I have created runner'dam to running routes with other fellow runners. Just choose which part of Amsterdam you would like to run in, and I will give you some recommendations.
